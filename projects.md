# PROJECTS

## R packages

### ```sanzo``` R Color Palettes Based on the Works of Sanzo Wada.

<a href="https://github.com/jmaasch/sanzo/" style="color: rgb(167,55,75)"><font color="A7374B">github.com/jmaasch/sanzo/</font></a>

Inspired by the art and color research of Sanzo Wada (1883-1967), his <a href="http://seigensha.com/en/2016/11/01/978-4-86152-247-5/" style="color: rgb(167,55,75)"><font color="A7374B">Dictionary Of Color Combinations</font></a>, and the incredible <a href="https://github.com/dblodorn/sanzo-wada" style="color: rgb(167,55,75)"><font color="A7374B">interactive site</font></a> by Dain M. Blodorn Kim, this package brings Wada's color combinations to R for easy use in data visualizations. This package honors 60 of Wada's color combinations: 20 duos, 20 trios, and 20 quads.
